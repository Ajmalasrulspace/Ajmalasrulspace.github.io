Football


1.Kedah FA


Darul Aman Stadium

First and foremost, I live in Kedah and born in Hospital Besar Alor Setar near to home ground of Kedah football team the Darul Aman Stadium. Along side with my father, I have visit various stadiums in Malaysia since I was 7 years old. I have visits Batu Kawan Stadium in Penang, National Stadium Bukit Jalil and Stadium Shah Alam in Selangor. I had watch the games for many times in Darul Aman Stadium and few time of Malaysia Cup or FA Cup final match. Being part of myself, Kedah will always be my favorite football team.

In 2013, I joins the Kedah Kenari Kid Academy, to grassroots academy that under the Kedah Football Association. It is the football academy that has contribute a few big name in the development of Kedah football teams. I joins the academy when I was 13 years old. I develop my skill in football in the academy almost two years.
